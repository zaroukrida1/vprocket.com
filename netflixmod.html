What is Netflix?

Netflix is an application for watching movies online that originated in the US. This streaming movie service was first launched in 1997 by Netflix INC and began offering a paid subscription plan in 1999.

The company previously specialized in DVD rentals of movies and other content. But after transforming into an app and operating entirely online, Netflix was one of the pioneers, quickly becoming one of the world’s largest, most prestigious, and quality streaming services.

Netflix is now available globally (except for some countries with strict communication policies such as China, North Korea).

What can you watch on Netflix?

Netflix has a huge and diverse movie library. On this app, you can find almost anything you need: movies, TV series, documentaries, reality shows, science movies, and more entertainment content.

Exclusive content is an excellent feature of this online movie-watching application. Many quality movies and shows produced by Netflix itself are only available on their platform. For example, the series Stranger Things, Money Heist, and others.

Every year Netflix releases dozens of movies, TV shows, and documentaries of high quality. These works often target user trends and feature famous directors, veteran actors, great scripts, and excellent cinematography.

The plus point is that Netflix has movies in all genres from various countries, including Asia, Europe, and Latin America.

The Experience of Watching Movies on Netflix

Watching movies on Netflix is smooth and impeccable:

  • Fast Movie Delivery: Netflix has global infrastructure, delivering movie content via CDN at lightning speed.
  • No Interruptions: The movie-watching experience is never interrupted by ads. Unlike YouTube, Netflix makes money from subscriptions, not ads.
  • Remember Where You Left Off: Netflix remembers where you left off, even if you stop watching and come back later.

Subtitles & Voiceovers

Netflix provides excellent subtitles and voiceover options. Subtitles are easy to read and run correctly, and are available in multiple languages.

Although voiceovers are more limited, they are generally complete for most content.
